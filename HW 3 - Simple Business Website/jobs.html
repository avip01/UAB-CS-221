Barista

As a Barista at our coffee bar, you will be the heart of our daily operations,
responsible for brewing coffee and crafting beverages that meet our quality standards.
You'll ensure every customer receives a warm welcome and a top-notch coffee experience.

Wage: $15 per hour

Tasks

  • Prepare and serve coffee and other beverages according to our recipes.
  • Maintain cleanliness and organization of the coffee bar area.
  • Manage inventory and restock supplies as needed.
  • Engage with customers, taking orders, and providing recommendations.
  • Operate the cash register and handle transactions.

Coffee Bar Supervisor

The Coffee Bar Supervisor oversees the barista team and ensures efficient operation of the coffee bar.
You will be responsible for training staff, managing schedules, and ensuring customer satisfaction remains high.

Wage: $22.50 per hour

Tasks

  • Supervise baristas and ensure they adhere to company standards.
  • Train new employees on coffee-making techniques and customer service.
  • Schedule shifts and manage daily operations.
  • Handle customer complaints and ensure a positive customer experience.
  • Oversee inventory management and order supplies as necessary.
